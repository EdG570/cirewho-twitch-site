About us

Fayye and Cirewho make up this married couple stream duo. Sharing their love for gaming is their main goal. Join them in their adventures. Share some laughs, tell some stories, and maybe watch them rage. Either way, it's sure to be a good time!

Fayye and CIREWHO started their gaming experience together in Rift. Raiding for a few months before moving on to other games. Eventually they moved on to games like: H1Z1, Rust, Wildstar, World of Warcraft and Overwatch.
